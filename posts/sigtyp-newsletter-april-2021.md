I am glad to be invited to contribute to [the SIGTYP April 2021 Newsletter](https://sigtyp.github.io/sigtyp-newsletter-Apr-2021.html).

SIGTYP is one of the special interest groups in the ACL community, which focuses on Language Typology. The work we introduced is about Drenjongke, the language spoken in Sikkim, India.

Before this work, our group was working mainly on the Drenjongke sound. The recordings collected during the past fieldwork can be downloaded upon request from the [website](https://phophono.aa-ken.jp/drenjongke.php).

Some audio files, namely nursery rhymes, are uploaded on YouTube along with their lyrics and English translation. We are now working on developing a post-correction model of the OCR for Drenjongke. Stay tuned!
