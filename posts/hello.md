Hi, this is Mana. I am a 2nd-year master's student in the Faculty of Systems Design at Tokyo Metropolitan University, advised by Mamoru Komachi.

Before coming to TMU, I received a Bachelor of Arts (Linguistics major) at International Christian University. To put it in a nutshell, I study how language models behave.

My interests are in the language, how a human acquires it, or a machine, say, a neural network learns something out of it. I am also engaging in the fairness of Artificial Intelligence by examining the potential adversarial consequences of AI usage against disadvantaged groups.
