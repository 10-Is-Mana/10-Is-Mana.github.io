Have you noticed "Website Carbon Badge" on my website's home? As one of the researchers who are working in the AI related field, I am aware that the increasing use of computational resources for training models is a huge problem.

It is often the case for any things that the problems of sustainability or of ethics is less concerned than performances, so as the case of the web (which is another point that is pretty interesting and important, but I am not going deeper this time.).

Even though the things that I can do are limited, here as a one of such way, I put the Badge to raise people's awareness. Another thing I adopted is using Ecosia for my default browser. I will let you know as I found other ways to "engage" in this issue.
